# Code of Conduct

Air Assist follows the
[**Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

By participating in this project — filing issues, opening pull
requests, commenting in discussions, or contributing in any other way
— you agree to abide by its terms.

## TL;DR

- Be respectful. Disagreement is fine; personal attacks, harassment,
  and discrimination are not.
- Assume good faith. Most people you disagree with online are trying
  to solve the same problem you are.
- Keep the bar technical. Focus comments on code, design, and
  behaviour, not on the person who wrote them.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates the Covenant,
please contact the project maintainer directly:

- **Email:** sjschillinger@gmail.com

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially. We
will not retaliate against good-faith reporters, and we will not
publish reporter identities without explicit consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban contributors
whose behaviour violates this Code. Enforcement follows the
[Covenant's enforcement guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines).
